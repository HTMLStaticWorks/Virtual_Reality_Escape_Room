THE RULEBOOK

TOURNAMENT RULES

01.All participants must have an active NeonEscape account and signed waiver before the event day.
02.Teams of 2–4 players compete in the same designated mission for that month's tournament.
03.Escape time is recorded automatically by the arena system. Game Masters validate all official times.
04.Hint usage is tracked. Teams with the same escape time are ranked by fewest hints used.
05.No outside props, devices, or audio communication with non-team members during the mission.
06.Disputes must be raised with the Game Master immediately post-session. No retroactive appeals.
07.Prize distribution within 7 days of the event via UPI / bank transfer.
