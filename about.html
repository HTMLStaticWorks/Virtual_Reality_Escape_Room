THE ORIGIN

WHY WE BUILT THIS

In 2022, founders Karthik Nair and Priya Mehta returned from an international VR gaming expo with one obsession: bringing untethered VR entertainment to Tier-2 India. Physical escape rooms had limits — broken locks, reset times, and the same puzzles over and over. Virtual Reality had none of those limits.

They chose Rourkela because they believed every city deserved world-class immersive entertainment — not just the metros. Six months, three prototypes, and one ambitious lease later, NeonEscape VR was born.

Today we operate 6 simultaneous VR stations with 12 missions, and new worlds are added every quarter. The goal remains the same: blow your mind and make you come back.

THE AESTHETIC

THE 90s CYBERPUNK SOUL

Everything about NeonEscape is intentionally retro-futuristic. The neon signs, the VHS-grain textures on the lobby screens, the Orbitron font on every surface — it's all a love letter to 90s sci-fi and arcade culture.

We drew deep inspiration from Tron, Johnny Mnemonic, and the neon-drenched arcades of the early 90s. The design philosophy: you should feel like you stepped into a cyberpunk film even before the headset goes on.

YOUR SAFETY

SAFETY & HYGIENE

Your wellbeing is our mission priority. Every session is backed by rigorous safety protocols.

UV HEADSET SANITATION

Every headset is sanitized with medical-grade UV-C light and antimicrobial wipes between each session. Hygiene logs are kept.

PADDED PLAY ZONES

Every play area has foam-padded walls, soft floor tiles, and a clear 2.5m x 2.5m safe zone per player. No sharp edges.

DIGITAL WAIVER

All players complete a digital liability waiver before their session. Parents must countersign for players under 16.

MOTION SICKNESS PROTOCOL

Sessions can be paused or ended at any time. We have a designated calm-down zone with cold water and dim lighting.

GLASSES FRIENDLY

Our headsets accommodate most prescription glasses. IPD adjustable for all face shapes. Soft face gaskets included.

FIRST AID READY

All Game Masters are first-aid certified. Full first aid kit on premises. Emergency protocols posted at every station.
