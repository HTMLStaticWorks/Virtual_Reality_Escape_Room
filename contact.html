GOT QUESTIONS?

FREQUENTLY ASKED

Motion sickness affects a small percentage of players, typically in newer VR users. We choose room-scale experiences with minimal artificial locomotion to minimize this. You can pause or exit your session at any time. We have a recovery zone with cold water and seating. If you have a history of motion sickness, let us know before booking and we'll recommend gentler missions.

Yes. Our Meta Quest Pro headsets accommodate most prescription glasses. The IPD (interpupillary distance) is fully adjustable, and the face gasket has enough depth for standard frames. Very thick or wide-temple glasses may not fit — if unsure, call us in advance for advice.

Our general minimum age is 10 years. Players under 13 must be accompanied by a parent or guardian who also plays. Horror-rated missions (Haunted Mainframe) are strictly 16+. Family-friendly missions like Neon Temple and Glitch City are suitable for all ages. Each room's page lists specific age guidance.

Most VR missions are 60 minutes. The Deep Signal mission is 75 minutes. Add 15–20 minutes for the pre-game briefing and gear fitting, and 10 minutes post-game for stats review. Budget about 90 minutes total for a single standard mission visit.

Yes! Glitch City is designed for solo and small group play. A few other missions also support 1 player. However, most missions are designed for 2–6 players and are significantly more fun with a team. We also offer a "matchmaking" service on weekends — tell us you're solo and we'll pair you with other walk-in players.

Advance booking is strongly recommended, especially for weekends and evenings — slots fill up 48–72 hours ahead. Walk-ins are welcome on weekday afternoons when availability exists. To guarantee your slot and preferred mission, book online via our Rooms page. Groups of 5+ should always pre-book.
